"AI is expensive" was true in 2023. In 2026, the landscape has flipped — you can run a full content workflow, design assets, and even edit video for under $50/mo total across 3-4 tools. Here's every cheap plan worth your time.

## 🏆 Best Overall: <span class="price-badge">$12/mo</span> — Simplified Pro

Simplified gives you AI writing, design, social scheduling, and brand kits in one dashboard. The Pro plan at **$12/mo** covers what would cost $40+ across separate tools. Best for solo creators and small teams who want one tool instead of five.

> Verdict: Best value for money in 2026. The all-in-one approach means fewer subscriptions to manage.

## 📝 Cheapest AI Writer: <span class="price-badge">$9/mo</span> — Copy.ai

Copy.ai's Individual plan dropped to $9/mo in early 2026. It handles blog posts, social captions, emails, and ad copy. Limitations: no plagiarism checker, no long-form editor as polished as Jasper. But at $9/mo, it's the cheapest dedicated AI writer that's actually good.

## 🎨 Best Budget Image AI: <span class="price-badge">$0/mo</span> — Canva Free (with AI)

Canva's free tier now includes 50 AI image generations per month using their Magic Media tool. For $0, it's the cheapest image AI by far. Limitations: limited to 50/month, no SDXL/FLUX quality. For heavier users, VEED's free tier includes basic AI video and image features.

## 🎬 Cheapest Video AI: <span class="price-badge">$12/mo</span> — VEED Pro

VEED's Pro plan at $12/mo includes AI subtitles (99% accuracy), auto-reframing for social, and noise reduction. Industry standard for browser-based editing with AI features baked in.

## 🗣️ Best Budget Voice AI: <span class="price-badge">$5/mo</span> — ElevenLabs Starter

ElevenLabs Starter at $5/mo gives you 100,000 characters/month of AI voice generation. Natural-sounding TTS, voice cloning, and dubbing. If you create any audio content, this is the cheapest quality voice AI.

## 📋 Cheapest Meeting AI: <span class="price-badge">$0/mo</span> — MeetGeek Free

MeetGeek's free tier records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings up to 3 hours/month. Pushes highlights to Slack and Notion. For occasional meeting notes, free is perfect.
